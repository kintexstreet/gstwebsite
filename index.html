Our Journey Through Time

Explore the pivotal moments and innovations that have defined GST's legacy in safety and technology.

1986

The Genesis

Geumgang Engineering Company is founded, laying the groundwork for future innovation.

1989

Formal Incorporation

The company officially registers as a Corporate entity: Geumgang E&I Co., Ltd.

1992

Forging Alliances: Keystone

A significant Representation Agreement is signed with Keystone - Anderson Greenwood Corporations (AGCO).

1997

Strengthening Ties: Tyco

The partnership evolves with a Representation Agreement involving Tyco - Anderson Greenwood Corporations (AGCO).

Context: Anderson Greenwood and Crosby merged operations in 1998.

2000

Expanding Portfolio

New representation agreements established with VAREC & WHESSOE, broadening market reach.

2002

Partnering with FIKE

A strategic Representation Agreement is secured with FIKE Corporations, enhancing safety solutions.

2010

A New Identity: GST

The company embraces its modern identity, renaming to "Geumgang S&T Co., Ltd." (GST).

2012

Pentair Partnership

Representation Agreement formed with Pentair - Anderson Greenwood Crosby (AGC).

2018

Collaboration with EMERSON

GST becomes a Local Business Partner through an agreement with EMERSON.

2022

Certified Service Excellence

Achieved registration as an EMERSON SSP (Official Service Provider), validating service capabilities.

2023

Industry Recognition

Honored with the Emerson Best LBP award for significant contributions to decarbonation efforts.

Valve Repair & Maintenance Services

Expert Solutions at Our Dangjin Service Center

Welcome to the GST Korea Service Center, where precision engineering meets unparalleled expertise. We understand that the integrity of your operations hinges on the reliability of your critical valve components. Our Dangjin facility is equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by certified technicians dedicated to restoring your equipment to OEM specifications.

We are committed to delivering dependable solutions through rigorous pre-delivery quality control, ensuring peace of mind and operational excellence for your most demanding industrial applications.
